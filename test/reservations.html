Reservation Rules

Please read carefully before making a reservation

1. Booking Policy

All reservations must be made through this online system. Walk-ins are accepted but reservations have priority.

2. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours before your scheduled appointment. Repeated no-shows may result in booking restrictions.

3. Late Arrival

Please arrive on time. If you're more than 5 minutes late, your reservation may be cancelled and your seat given to walk-in customers.

4. Payment

Payment is due upon completion of service. We accept cash and major credit cards.

5. Service Duration

Estimated service times are provided as guidance. Actual duration may vary depending on hair type and chosen services.

6. Barber Assignment

While you can choose your preferred barber, we reserve the right to reassign in case of emergencies or barber unavailability.

7. Hygiene Requirements

All clients must maintain proper hygiene. We reserve the right to refuse service if hygiene standards are not met.

8. Children Under 12

Children under 12 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian throughout the service.

9. Service Modifications

Add-ons or service changes may be requested during your appointment, subject to barber availability and time constraints.

10. Rescheduling

If we need to reschedule your appointment (e.g., barber emergency), we will contact you immediately to find an alternative time.
